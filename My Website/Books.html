Randy has published few books, but is writing many. The current titles you will see in the market are Destiny Calls, and soon, The Second Island. He will shortly begin the commencement of a new series, which won't be available to the public until at least 2024.


In Destiny Calls, the main characters are Todd and Keyanne, the other most important characters being Courtney, Hannah, Ben, Corra, Catherine, and Emma to name a few.

The first book of the series is about Todd and Keyanne aiding the escape of the Shrinking and Growing Animals from the first Kauru Kaumatra island. Todd is the son of Courtney and David Bradford, who are both in the beginning scenes of the book. Keyanne is the daughter of Shannon and Tom Seraneph.

Todd grew up around Corra, the main Shrinking and Growing Animal of the book, but was soon introduced to others, namely Catherine, Ruby, and Onyxis. The escape of the island is planned while the invasion of the island is commencing by King Callidus, the Antagonist.


In The Second Island, a new main character is introduced. Her name is Grace, and she has the unique ability to control heat. Shortly after the beginning of this book, she is taken with Todd and Keyanne back to the second island, where they engage in the dangerous conflict between the natives of the island, and the Shrinking and Growing Animals.
